Academic Excellence

Our integrated curriculum blends Islamic wisdom with modern sciences, preparing students for academic success and ethical leadership in today's world.

Our Academic Programs

A balanced education that develops both spiritual grounding and intellectual prowess through comprehensive programs

Islamic Studies

Comprehensive program covering Quranic studies, Hadith, Fiqh, Islamic history, and Arabic language to develop deep understanding of Islamic principles.

  • Quran Memorization & Tajweed
  • Islamic Jurisprudence (Fiqh)
  • Prophetic Traditions (Hadith)
  • Arabic Language & Literature
  • Islamic History & Civilization

Science & Technology

Modern science education integrated with Islamic principles, emphasizing hands-on experimentation, research, and ethical applications of technology.

  • Physics, Chemistry & Biology
  • Computer Science & Programming
  • Mathematics & Statistics
  • Laboratory Research Projects
  • Science & Islamic Ethics

Languages & Humanities

Developing communication skills and cultural understanding through comprehensive language programs and social sciences.

  • English Language & Literature
  • Assamese & Regional Languages
  • Social Studies & History
  • Comparative Religion Studies
  • Communication Skills

Professional Development

Practical skills and career-oriented programs that prepare students for professional success while maintaining Islamic values.

  • Leadership & Management
  • Public Speaking & Debate
  • Research Methodology
  • Computer Applications
  • Entrepreneurship Skills
