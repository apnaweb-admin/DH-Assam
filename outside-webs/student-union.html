About SIDRA

SIDRA stands for Students' Initiative for Development, Rights & Advocacy. We are the official student union representing all undergraduate and graduate students at our university.


Founded in 2010, our mission is to foster a supportive and inclusive campus environment where every student can thrive academically, socially, and personally. We work tirelessly to protect student rights, organize developmental programs, and advocate for positive changes in university policies.


Through various committees and initiatives, we address issues ranging from academic support and mental health to campus sustainability and social justice. Our union is run by students, for students, ensuring that your voice is always heard.

Our Initiatives

Academic Support

Free tutoring, study groups, and workshops to help students excel in their courses. We also advocate for fair grading policies and accessible learning resources.

Mental Health Advocacy

Promoting mental wellness through counseling referrals, stress-relief events, and awareness campaigns. We work to reduce stigma around mental health issues.

Campus Sustainability

Leading green initiatives including recycling programs, tree planting drives, and advocating for environmentally-friendly campus policies.
