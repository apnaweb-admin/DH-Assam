About DHIU Assam Campus

DHIU Assam Campus is situated at Baisha-Barmara in Barpeta District of Assam. It was established on 24 March 2013 as the first off-campus of DHIU Kerala in North East India.

Key Facts

  • Established: 24 March 2013
  • Location: Baisha-Barmara, Barpeta District, Assam
  • Campus Area: About 10 acres of land
  • Nearest Station: Barpeta Road Railway station (43 km)
  • Airport: Guwahati Airport (80 km)

Campus Facilities

  • Academic Block
  • Attached Hostel Building
  • Jamia Masjid
  • Darul Huda Model School
  • Alif Preschool
  • Hadia Barpeta Regional Office
