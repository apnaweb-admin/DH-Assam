Admissions Process

Begin your journey towards excellence in Islamic and modern education. Our admissions process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our nurturing learning environment.

Join Our Educational Journey

We welcome applications from motivated students who are eager to learn and grow in an environment that blends Islamic values with modern education. Our admissions process seeks to identify students who will contribute to and benefit from our collaborative learning community.

Eligibility Criteria for Admission

Age Requirements

  • Preparatory Year: 5-6 years
  • First Year: 6-7 years
  • Second Year: 7-8 years
  • Third Year: 8-9 years
  • Fourth Year: 9-10 years
  • Fifth Year: 10-11 years

Academic Requirements

  • Previous year report card
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Character Certificate from previous institution
  • Birth Certificate for new admissions
  • Pass certificate of previous class

Documents Required

  • 4 passport size photographs
  • Aadhar Card copy (student & parents)
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Income certificate (for fee concession)
  • Residence proof
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)

Important Notes

Admission test and interview are mandatory for all applicants.

Fee structure varies based on class and category (regular/boarding).

Sibling discount of 10% available for second child from same family.

Special consideration for orphans and economically weaker sections with proper documentation.

Admission Process Timeline

  1. Application Submission: Complete the online application form with all required documents. Applications are accepted from September 1st each year.
  2. Document Verification: Our admissions office will verify all submitted documents. Ensure all certificates and proofs are authentic and up-to-date.
  3. Entrance Assessment: All applicants must appear for an entrance test covering basic Islamic knowledge, language skills, and general aptitude.
  4. Personal Interview: Shortlisted candidates and their parents/guardians will be invited for a personal interview with the admissions committee.
  5. Admission Decision: Successful candidates will receive admission offers within two weeks of the interview. Decisions are based on overall assessment.
  6. Fee Payment & Enrollment: Complete the enrollment process by paying the admission fee and submitting remaining documents within the specified timeframe.
