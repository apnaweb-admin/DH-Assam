Pioneering Islamic Education in Assam

Darul Huda Assam Centre, established in March 2013 at Baisha-Barmara in Barpeta District, stands as the first off-campus extension of Darul Huda Islamic University, Kerala in North East India. Spanning over 10 acres of dedicated educational space, our institution represents a harmonious blend of traditional Islamic wisdom and contemporary academic excellence.

As a beacon of knowledge and cultural preservation, we are committed to nurturing well-rounded individuals who can contribute meaningfully to both their faith community and the broader society. Our campus serves not just as an educational institution, but as a vibrant center for community development and cultural revitalization.

Our Mission & Values

Guiding principles that shape our educational philosophy and community

Our Mission

Our mission can be viewed from six dimensions:

Dynamic and Dutiful

To develop an Islamic educational framework to prepare religious scientists ('Ulama) who acquire, practice and propagate Islam, being alert of their ultimate responsibilities towards Almighty Allah and towards themselves, family, institution, society, surroundings and private and public resources.

Holistic and Harmonious

To offer a holistic and comprehensive material education, harmonious with the Islamic principles to foster personal, spiritual and intellectual development.

Integrated Education

To incorporate Islamic doctrinal beliefs and values into educational practices, integrating theories with practices, and extending classroom activities with real-life situations.

Unified Knowledge

To unify the purpose of seeking knowledge of various contents and nature in line with the Islamic legacy of hierarchy and unity of knowledge.

Excellence and Innovation

To provide academic excellence of highest standards and develop new trends and ideas to keep with the modernization of knowledge.

Productive and Self-Reliant

To offer evidence-based academic tools of wisdom to produce better material and build up self-reliance to meet academic challenges.

Our Core Values

These fundamental values guide every aspect of life at Darul Huda Assam Centre. We believe in nurturing ethical leaders who contribute positively to society while maintaining strong Islamic identity.

  • Curiosity: Fostering intellectual inquiry and lifelong learning
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and ethical principles in all endeavors
  • Compassion: Demonstrating empathy and care for all creation
  • Resilience: Developing strength to overcome challenges with faith
  • Innovation: Encouraging creative thinking within Islamic framework
  • Community: Building strong bonds and serving society
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in all pursuits

Our Vision

DHIU aims to be the bastion of expertise in Islamic education restoring the dynamic role and educational superiority of past Muslim society in all intellectual programs and that seeks to rekindle the spirit of scholarship in Islamic sciences.

Islamization of Knowledge

Our vision includes Islamization of knowledge and setting up international research centers of educational excellence in various Islamic disciplines for the development of Muslim community in learning, teaching and research of highest standards.

Exemplary Institution

We aim to be an exemplary institution which can mold up a bunch of such religious scientists (Ulamas) who are the virtual heirs of the holy prophet Muhammad (pbuh), equipped to face modern challenges with traditional wisdom.

Global Impact

To establish ourselves as a globally recognized center for Islamic scholarship that bridges traditional knowledge with contemporary needs, creating solutions for modern challenges while preserving our rich heritage.
